How to Operate - User Guide

  • Step 1: Setup Profile
    Go to Settings to update your Company Details. This info will appear on every PDF invoice.
  • Step 2: Add Parties
    Add your clients and suppliers in the Account Manager to maintain your digital directory.
  • Step 3: Create Invoices
    Generate digital invoices, add items, and click Save to keep a permanent record.
  • Step 4: Record Payments
    Use Receipt & Pay to record every transaction and keep your cash flow accurate.
  • Step 5: View Records & PDF
    Access Saved Records to view past invoices and send direct PDFs to your clients.
  • Step 6: Real-time Ledgers
    Check View Ledger to see detailed statements and outstanding balances of any party.
  • Step 7: Backup & Transfer
    Download a Backup to your mobile and easily Restore it on any other device.
